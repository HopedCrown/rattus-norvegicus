This adapting creature also ingests a _**wide**_ variety of foods, and is therefore **internal heterotrophs**. They even eat other rats! 
They can eat:
- Your garbage
- Your recycling
- Your compost
- Fruits
- Vegetables
- nuts
- cereals
- fish
- rats (as aforementioned)
- lizards
- chickens
- much more we don't know about

**And people say *humans* eat a lot!**
