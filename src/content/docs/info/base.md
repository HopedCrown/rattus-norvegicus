This deadly (and ghastly) pest is a vertebrate, but that doesn't mean it's our friend. Sadly, the animal doesn't know that, and _almost_ anywhere humans can live, **it** can live. This pesky rat has Bilateral Symmetry and is a Coelomate. It also is of ***Least Concern*** on all Ecological Conservation Status readings. Attempts at extermination have failed for this species.
This species is also endothermic, as expected of vertebrates.
